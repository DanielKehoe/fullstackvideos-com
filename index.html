Fullstack Video Tutorials from Daniel Kehoe and AsiaG

Asia Granada is known to the world as AsiaG. She recorded these videos to share with other learners as she began learning to code in 2016. She has a talent for demanding explanations and presenting the answers in a way anyone can understand.

Daniel Kehoe is the author of the popular book, Learn Ruby on Rails. AsiaG enlisted him to help on these videos, answering AsiaG's questions and editing the scripts for accuracy and detail.

Together, AsiaG and Daniel Kehoe are on a mission to make application development accessible to everyone. The video tutorials are for entrepreneurs, career changers, and anyone else who wants to learn to code.
